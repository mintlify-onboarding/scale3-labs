---
title: FireHydrant
description: Steps to setup alerts for FireHydrant
---

1. Navigate to FireHydrant [bot users page](https://app.firehydrant.io/organizations/bots).

![FireHydrant bot creating page](/images/firehydrant-bot-creating-page.png)

2. Select **Create bot**
3. Enter the **name** and **description** of your bot
4. Click **Create bot user**
5. Copy the **token** for your bot and save it (this will not be shown again).
6. Navigate to [Scale3 Labs Integrations](https://www.scale3labs.com/autopilot/settings/integrations)
7. Paste your **Bot Token** into the box titled "Bot Token (API Key)" under the **FireHydrant** section and click **Connect**

![Firehydrant Bot Token](/images/firehydrant-bot-token.png)

8. You should now see the 'Disconnect' button under FireHydrant!

![Disconnect](/images/firehydrant-disconnect.png)
